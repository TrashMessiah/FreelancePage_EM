Few words about me

Psychological help, intervention during life crisis, individual pshychotherapy led in Gestalt methodology

///nowy tekst

I've graduated from psychology at University of Adam Mickiewicz in Poznań, currently I'm going through the certification process on Great Poland School of Gestalt Psychotherapy. I've also graduated from the 2 years school of systemic and family therapy in Great Poland Association of Systemic Therapy I also went through many clinical internships. I broaden my knowledge and experience by participation in developmental and psychotherapeutic workshops and training. I submit my work to constant supervision. In my work I stick to Ethical Code of Polish Association of Gestalt Psychotherapy.

I have many years of experience in helping people in search of their problems. My work experience is primarily tied to accompanying people in their development process and discovery of resources - strengths and fields, which can pose an obstacle in living life to its fullest.

I am a firm believer that thanks to our consciousness we reach our very needs and gain the skills necessary to satisfy them as well as shaping the sorrounding reality, one which we really crave for.
