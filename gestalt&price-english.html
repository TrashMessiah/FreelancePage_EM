Therapeutic consultation

It is a meeting, which aims at preliminary identification of living and emotional situation of the patient, thanks to that it is possible to choose the most adequate form of help. The amount of consultation session is dependent on the complexity of the issue and readiness of the customer to take on next steps. It is however usually between 2-3 meetings.

Individual psychotherapy

It is a cycle of regular meetings aiming at permanent change in day to day functioning of customer, recognition of the resources and limitations on a way to fulfilling and satisfying life. Psychotherapy may have a short-term perspective(up to 20 meetings) or long-term. The long-term therapy is a process of deeper cooperation which affects the functioning of the patient on many levels. The pace of the therapy is adjusted to the patient needs and lasts as long as there is a need for it. Sessions are held cyclically, once in a week, and last for 50 minutes.

Workshops of personal development

Group meeting conducted in Gestalt current, based on group process focusing participants around broadening consciousness in scope of emotion, body, thoughts and the ways of living in terms of relations. Group work allows for assessment free contact with self and the others and allows for safe environment where you can experience and test your borders, seek help in times of need and inspire each other.

Gestalt

Psychotherapeutic work in Gestalt methodology is all about broadening self awareness through meeting in the ambiance of authenticity and truth with the therapist. It allows for evaluation of self in relation/world, experiencing true self in authentic emotions, the renewal of feeling, naming, sharing, assimilation and acceptance of what is mine.

During the work the emotions meet, thoughts an needs as well as the feedback coming from the body. Thanks to the consciousness and experiencing self here and now we are allowed to see the possibility of making choice in agreement with what we need ad the choice of what and how we want for us. It allows for taking the full responsibility for self as well as the creation of life in a full and satisfying way.

For me personally, the key aspect of Gestalt approach is the faith in the capability of individual, the ability to support self and making full use of ones own potential.
